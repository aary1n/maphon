## Systematic traps (cross-cutting, all verified in-file)
 
1. **Angular vs /2ПЂ units** вЂ” W20 prints rad sвЃ»В№ as "Hz" for g_s, Оє_s, Оє_c; CP prints /2ПЂ. See header check. This is the single most likely silent bug in a QuantumCumulants.jl port.
2. **Fitted values masquerading as constants downstream** вЂ” CP Table I cites [40]=W20 for all six k/Оі rates, but those are W20's *maser-fitted* row, not the error-barred TR-EPR row. Provenance bottoms out in a fit, not a measurement.
3. **High-field numbers in zero-field models** вЂ” Sloop 1981 (X-band, ~0.3 T) supplies P ratios (everyone), П„_Z (O12), and SLR/decay rates (B15) for zero-field devices. O12 itself flags that its zero-field fits differ from Sloop's high-field values by 2Г— (Tв‚‚) and "several" Г— (П„_Z/П„_X) (p. 19).
4. **Citation laundering** вЂ” "в‰€4 MHz" (W20) в†ђ "a few MHz" (O12); "1.4495 GHz [15]" (W20) в†ђ B15, which measured 1.4493; "9.5:2:1 [4,5]" (L25) в†ђ secondaries, primary is Sloop.
5. **Reference-plane mismatch in pump powers** вЂ” laser-exit (O12) vs absorbed (B15) vs LC-output (W20) vs launched (L25).
